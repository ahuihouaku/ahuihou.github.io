🌈 About Me

Aloha, my name is Kirk, and I’ve been passionate about helping people for as long as I can remember. My very first job in high school was about supporting students — and that focus has followed me through every role I’ve had: part-time teacher, college planning advisor, college instructor, and more.

Travel has also been a huge part of my life. I’ve explored places like Italy, France, Germany, Canada, Korea, and especially Japan. I bring that same love for local culture into every trip I plan and every lesson I teach.

I created Aloha Insider so travelers — especially those from Japan — can experience the Hawaiʻi they dream of. Whether you’re visiting solo, with friends, or with kids, I’ll help you avoid tourist traps and plan something memorable.
