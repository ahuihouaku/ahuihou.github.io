🌈 About Me

Aloha, my name is Kirk, and I’ve been passionate about helping people for as long as I can remember. My very first job in high school was about supporting students — and that focus has followed me through every role I’ve had: part-time teacher, college planning advisor, college instructor, and more.

Travel has also been a huge part of my life. I’ve been exploring the world since I was a baby, but it was visiting places like Italy, France, Germany, Canada, Korea, and especially Japan that gave me a deeper understanding of what it means to be a guest in someone else’s home.

I created Aloha Insider to help travelers — especially those from Japan — experience the Hawaiʻi I know and love. Whether it’s your first visit or your tenth, I’m here to help you make the most of your trip, with local insights, cultural etiquette, and practical tips.
