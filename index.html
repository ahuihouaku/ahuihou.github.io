🌈 About Me

Aloha, my name is Kirk, and I’ve been passionate about helping people for as long as I can remember. My very first job in high school was about supporting students — and that focus has followed me through every role I’ve had: part-time teacher, college planning advisor, college instructor, and more. Whether it’s guiding someone through their education or helping them feel confident in a new place, I’ve always loved being part of someone’s journey.

Travel has also been a huge part of my life. I’ve been exploring the world since I was a baby, but it was visiting places like Italy, France, Germany, Canada, Korea, and especially Japan that gave me a deeper understanding of what it means to be a guest in someone else’s home. I loved learning about local customs, trying new foods, and seeing how culture shapes daily life — and I bring that same mindset to helping visitors experience Hawaiʻi.

I created Aloha Insider because I want travelers — especially those visiting from Japan — to have the kind of experience they dream about when they imagine Hawaiʻi. Yes, it’s a beautiful place, but like any destination, there are things people might not know: high prices, tourist traps, tipping confusion, unsafe areas, or spending time and money in places that aren’t worth it.

As a parent, I’ve also discovered a whole new side of travel — one I never fully appreciated before. I now know how important it is to plan trips that include zoos, aquariums, safe beaches, playgrounds, and quiet family moments. I’ve traveled for nightlife with friends, cultural exploration, and now as a family. Because of that, I understand the different ways people experience Hawaiʻi, and I can help plan the perfect trip for any kind of traveler — whether it’s solo, with friends, or with kids in tow.
