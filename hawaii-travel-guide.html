🍱 Don't Bring Airline Food Off the Plane

If you're flying internationally, do not bring any leftover food off the plane. Customs may question you. Eat it or leave it onboard. Don’t worry – I can help you find anything similar once you’re here!

💵 Tipping in Hawaii

Tipping is out of control in the U.S. – some places suggest 25% or more! Never feel pressured to tip more than 20%, and that’s only for exceptional service. 15–18% is fine. No need to tip when picking up takeout unless you really want to.

🚫 Be Wary of People Approaching You

If someone offers you a ride or service on the spot, be cautious. Politely say you already have plans. It might be a scam or overpriced service.

🏨 Hotel Fees & Tips

Even after you book online, many hotels add daily resort fees. Tip a couple of dollars per day for room cleaning – more at the start of your stay may even improve your service!

🚗 Transportation Pros & Cons

Buses aren't efficient like Japan. Rideshares may be hard to get in some areas. Parking can be tough and break-ins do happen. I offer trip planning to help you decide what's best for your needs.

🌧 Always Have a Rainy Day Backup

It does rain in paradise! I help you plan backups for outdoor activities so your day isn't wasted.

🍔 Food Hype vs. Local Gems

Just because a place was on TV doesn’t mean it’s still good. Ownership changes, prices rise. I’ll show you where the locals actually eat – and even help with budget spots, including how McDonald's can vary by location.

📍 Picking the Right Hotel Area

Hawaiʻi is small, but traffic is bad. Booking a hotel in the wrong area can ruin your trip. I’ll help you pick based on what you want to do – even if I don’t book it for you, I can guide you.

📅 Avoid Busy Tourist Times

Weekends and holidays get packed. I help you avoid peak hours and plan smarter – go to popular spots on weekdays and book chill things on weekends.

⛽ Gas & Parking Tips

Gas is expensive in Hawaiʻi. Knowing when and where to fill up saves money. Beach parking can be crowded – I help you plan that too.

🌊 Safety First

Hawaiʻi is beautiful but can be dangerous. Some beaches have strong currents. Hiking trails can be risky. Even some so-called historical sites are tourist traps. I'll help you know what's worth your time and what to avoid.
