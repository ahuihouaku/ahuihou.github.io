🍱 Don't Bring Airline Food Off the Plane

International flyers: don’t bring food off the plane! Eat it or leave it. I can help you find your favorite snacks locally.

💵 Tipping in Hawaii

Tip 15–20% max for great service. No need to overtip. No tip needed for takeout orders.

🚫 Be Wary of People Approaching You

If approached with offers (rides, events), kindly decline. Some are scams or overpriced.

🏨 Hotel Fees & Tips

Expect resort fees. Tip housekeeping daily – especially at the start. Tip a few dollars for extra towels or requests.

🚗 Transportation Pros & Cons

Buses = slow. Rideshare = limited in rural spots. Parking is tough. I’ll help plan your route efficiently.

🌧 Rainy Day Backup

It rains in Hawaiʻi! I help plan indoor alternatives to avoid wasted days.

🍔 Food Hype vs. Local Gems

TV spots can be overrated or overpriced. I’ll recommend where locals really eat.

📍 Choosing the Right Hotel Area

Don’t just book anywhere – traffic is real. I’ll guide you to areas that match your goals and budget.

📅 Avoid Busy Tourist Times

Weekend crowds are intense. I help plan weekday visits to top attractions.

⛽ Gas & Parking

Gas = $$$. Parking = limited. I’ll help you navigate smarter.

🌊 Safety First

Not all beaches or hikes are safe. Some “historic sites” are tourist traps. I’ll tell you what’s worth your time.
